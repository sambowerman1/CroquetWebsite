Roslyn Considers Women
Friday, July 26, 2024
Roslyn's recent DEI summit has sparked the question, should women be allowed to compete at Roslyn National Croquet Club. For the past past 112 years, Roslyn National has been an exclusive men's and white's only club. Now Roslyn considers abandoning their heritage for a new era of inclusivity.


The result of the preliminary voting do not look optimistic for the girls. In a vote held by Elliot "The Fuhrer" Grucella went as follows:

Matthew Kramer: "Nay"
Connor Crilly: "Nay"
Sam Bowerman: "Nay"
Trevor Mills: "Yay"
Elliot Grucella: "Nay"
Now we hold and breath and wait as the future of Roslyn National is still to be decided.
